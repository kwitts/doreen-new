About

Doreen understands that your appearance is important to you and makes it her goal to address your needs and concerns to ensure that you leave feeling better about yourself and your hair. Doreen listens to each and every client to discover their lifestyle, time constraints, and concerns they may have. After assessing your needs, she'll provide a cut, color, or style that will make your hair the best it can be.

Doreen's Story

Growing up being the middle daughter with one older sister and one younger sister I soon began to be envious of their straight hair, feeling frustrated with my unruly curls and frizz. I felt unaccepted, less-than and ugly. If you have ever felt those feelings, you are not alone.

I found Deva Curl over 5 years ago and realized curly and unruly hair was soon to become my passion, I have lived with curly, unruly hair and have now created a passion for curly hair. My passion for the last five years has been to become a curly haired Deva Curl expert, which I have accomplished through ongoing education, investing time, finances and the willingness to commit to those standards. Investing in myself to bring fresh, current, precise techniques in order t to give you the best for your investment of time and money.

Why make an appointment with me, when there are so many other stylists doing Deva Curl? Because of my passion, my driving force and standards, I will help you fall in love over and over with your curly hair. I live curly every day. Let me mentor you about hydrating your hair and create for you a custom shape and ease of maintenance with minimal effort on your part. Fall in love today!
